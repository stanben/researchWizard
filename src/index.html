sourceLink

Welcome to sourceLink! Select Sign In to familySearch to load your family information.

sourceLink is a tool used to view and evaluate sources of a person and his immediate family members.

Within the navagation header at the top of the web page is an information box which provides hints as what to do as well as an information summary when an action is performed. At times sourceLink will shorten words for names and places in order to fit the space available within the web page. When this word is selected, the information box will show the entire word.

The display area is divided into three regions. The square region at the bottom center is the Active Person Area. This region is surrounded at the left, top and right sides by the Attached Sources Area. The final region Possible Sources Area is adjacent to the left, top and right edges of the display area.

The Active Person Area begins at the top line with a colored shape identifiying the active person's gender followed by his full name. Background color for this area also reflects gender:

                 John Doe                  Mary Doe                  Chris Doe

Directly below the first line is that active person's vital information and familysearch record number. Below the reference person information and to the left is his spouse(s) and children of each spouse. To the right are similarly his parents and siblings. Marriage information is provided below each spouse and set of parents. Children and Sibling's last names are ignored if they are same last name as the father. Birth years and places are provided with each child and sibling.

The Attached Sources Area contains all sources that have been attached to the active person. The sources are ordered chronlogically from the earliest events (birth) to the latest events(death) in a clockwise direction within this area. Sources which are of the same event (such as birth, marriage and death records) are merged into a single source group. A source group title is the number of sources in the group followed by the type of records: 3 Marriage Records.

The Possible Sources Area as the name implies, contains sources which are not attached to the active person but are found to be possible sources for that person. Record Hints are not attached sources and would appear in this area marked by the record hint icon: .

Each piece of vital information in the Active Person Area is highlighted when the mouse passes over it. Clicking on a piece of vital information will select it. When selected, all sources in the Attached Sources Area and the Possible Sources Area comparable information, will display values that are highlited: confirm, partially confirm, or contradict the selected information. Clicking on a piece of vital information while holding down the Ctrl key will cause the person associated with this information to become the new Active person. All areas will be updated to reflect the information associated with the new active person.

Each source in the Attached Sources Area and Possible Sources Area is also highlighted when the mouse passes over it. Clicking on a piece of vital information will select it. When selected, the information contained within the selected source or group of sources will be displayed in tabular form. Clicking on a source while holding down the Ctrl key will cause the source to be highlited in a table along with all other sources that have information comparable to the selected source's information as well as comparable information of the person. All comparable information in the table will be highlited with confirm, partially confirm, or contradict marks.

In order to close an active table and return to the display area, click on the RETURN. button above the table.
